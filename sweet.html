our sweet options

Desert options

Baklava (Turkish): Layers of paper-thin phyllo pastry filled with crushed pistachios or walnuts, baked until golden, and drenched in a fragrant honey-lemon syrup.

Matcha Mochi Cake (Japanese): A delightful fusion dessert with a chewy, soft texture and the earthy, vibrant notes of premium green tea, often served with a dollop of red bean paste.

Crème Brûlée (French): A rich, silky vanilla bean custard topped with a brittle layer of caramelized sugar that shatters perfectly under the weight of a spoon.

Hot drink option

Masala Chai (Indian): Robust black tea simmered with whole milk and a "masala" of crushed ginger, green cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. It’s a hug in a mug.

Ethiopian Buna (Coffee): Traditional, dark-roasted Arabica coffee beans, hand-ground and brewed in a clay *jebena*. It is bold, earthy, and often served with a side of popcorn.

Italian Espresso Macchiato: A concentrated shot of intense, dark-roast espresso "marked" with a tiny cloud of velvety foamed milk for a smooth finish.

Cold drink option

Mango Lassi (Indian): A thick, creamy yogurt-based shake blended with sweet Alphonso mangoes and a tiny pinch of cardamom. It’s the ultimate cool-down for spicy food.

Lychee Sparkler (Chinese): A light and floral refreshment made with sweet lychee fruit, fresh lime juice, and sparkling soda water, garnished with mint leaves.

Turkish Ayran: A traditional, frothy yogurt drink whisked with cold water and a pinch of sea salt. It is incredibly refreshing and the perfect palate cleanser.
